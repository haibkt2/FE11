- Restaurant -

For over thirty years
Our owners Anna & Angelo have been serving great authentic Italian food to families in the Richmond area. We provide guests with a casual, friendly atmosphere and excellent service – in addition to the healthy portions of our tasteful food. We currently offer two locations to better serve you. The location in Sandston on Williamsburg Road is conveniently located near the Richmond International airport and has become a favorite place to dine for many travelers passing through Richmond as well as the community of Sandston. Our original location in Richmond on Nine Mile Road had been a family favorite since it opened in 1979.
